About Us

We Bring the Best Drinks to Your Business

At Opeyemi Stores, we are official distributors for top beverage companies including Nigerian Breweries Ltd, International Breweries Ltd, Guinness Ltd, Coca-Cola, 7Up Bottling Company Ltd and many other brands.

Our mission is simple: ensure every client gets exactly what they need, when they need it, at the best prices. We serve hotels, bars, restaurants, supermarkets, and individuals across Abeokuta and Ogun State.

  • Genuine and well-sourced products only
  • Wholesale and retail pricing available
  • Fast, reliable delivery to your location
  • Friendly and experienced sales team
  • Official distributor for Nigerian Breweries, Guinness, Coca-Cola & 7Up
